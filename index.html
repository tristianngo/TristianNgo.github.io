about me

Hi, my name is Tristian. I'm a senior at James Madison University studying Media Arts and Design with a concentration in Interactive Design and a minor in Computer Information Systems. I was once striving for something that I was not passionate about, trying to just go the route of money. I recently discovered something I enjoyed and was atleast was decent at which is UX/UI design. Media Arts and Design has taught me so much of broad range of design which sort of mixed in with being realistic with my career choices and pursueing something that I actually enjoyed.

I strive to be learn, create, and teach the more I move on in my career as a UX/UI designer. I am trying to take as much experience in so I am able to be the best I can be, but at the same time still enjoy the precious moments that life offers. I want to take those experiences and create things that come from only my brain and eventually share that knowledge with others.
